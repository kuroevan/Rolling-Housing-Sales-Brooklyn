Brooklyn Rolling Sales Analysis
This is an analysis on the Rolling Sales data for Brooklyn NYC from September 2015 - August 2016. 
Neighborhood Names and Descriptive Data as of 5/26/2016 
Building Class Category is based on Building Class at Time of Sale.

What we can realize after analyzing and cleaning this dataset is that the majority of homes sold in Brooklyn NYC between September 2015 - August 2016 had the following: 
cost of: 540,000 - 620,000 (US dollars)
size of: 3,100-3,500 (square feet)
